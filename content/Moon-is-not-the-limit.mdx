### My contribution:

## Mars Landing Level Design:

- Led the creation and sculpting of the terrain for the Mars landing level.
- Integrated high-quality textures to enhance realism and visual appeal.
- Implemented realistic environment lighting to immerse players in the game world.

## Hanger Level Design:

- Served as the level designer for the hanger section, contributing to its captivating gameplay and aesthetics.

## Dialogue System and QTE Testing:

- Thoroughly tested the dialogue system and Quick Time Events (QTE) functionality.
- Ensured seamless performance and adherence to design intentions.

## Comprehensive Game Testing:

- Conducted rigorous testing of the entire game to identify and address any issues, ensuring a smooth player experience.
- Meticulously eradicated bugs to enhance game stability.

## Valuable Bug Reporting:

- Assisted in enhancing the game by diligently reporting and documenting bugs found during testing.
- Collaborated with the development team to improve the overall gaming experience.

## Feature and Functionality Testing:

- Took charge of testing various game features and functionalities, ensuring their seamless integration and proper operation.

## Game Content Quality Assurance:

- Performed in-depth testing of game content, guaranteeing high-quality gameplay and uncovering any potential flaws.
